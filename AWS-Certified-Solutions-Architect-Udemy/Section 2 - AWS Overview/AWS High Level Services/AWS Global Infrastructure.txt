AWS Global Infrastructure
    - 19 regions and 57 Availability Zones - December 2018
    - 5 more regions and 15 more AZ's for 2019

Availability Zone
    - Data center with a whole bunch of servers
    - May be several data centres, but because they are close together, they are
      counted as 1 Availability zone

Region
    - Geographical area
    - Each region consists of 2 (or more) availability zones
